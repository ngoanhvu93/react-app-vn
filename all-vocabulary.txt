DANH SÁCH TẤT CẢ TỪ VỰNG (100 TỪ)

=== CHÀO HỎI (GREETINGS) ===
1. Hello - Xin chào (sin chow) - "Hello, how are you?"
2. Good morning - Chào buổi sáng (chow boo-ee sang) - "Good morning, everyone!"
3. Goodbye - Tạm biệt (tam bee-et) - "Goodbye, see you tomorrow!"

=== LỊCH SỰ (POLITENESS) ===
4. Thank you - Cảm ơn (cam uhn) - "Thank you for your help."
5. Please - Xin vui lòng (sin voo-ee long) - "Please help me."
6. Sorry - Xin lỗi (sin loy) - "I'm sorry for being late."

=== TÍNH TỪ (ADJECTIVES) ===
7. Beautiful - Đẹp (dep) - "What a beautiful day!"
8. Big - To (toh) - "This is a big house."
9. Small - Nhỏ (nyoh) - "This is a small car."
10. Good - Tốt (toht) - "You did a good job."
11. Bad - Xấu (sow) - "This is bad weather."
12. Hot - Nóng (nong) - "The weather is hot today."
13. Cold - Lạnh (lang) - "The water is cold."

=== THỨC ĂN & ĐỒ UỐNG (FOOD & DRINK) ===
14. Delicious - Ngon (ngon) - "This food is delicious."
15. Water - Nước (nook) - "I need some water."
16. Rice - Cơm (kum) - "I love eating rice."
17. Bread - Bánh mì (bang mee) - "I eat bread for breakfast."
18. Milk - Sữa (soo-ah) - "I drink milk every day."
19. Coffee - Cà phê (kah feh) - "I drink coffee in the morning."
20. Tea - Trà (trah) - "I like drinking tea."
21. Beer - Bia (bee-ah) - "I drink beer with friends."

=== SỐ ĐẾM (NUMBERS) ===
22. One - Một (moht) - "I have one apple."
23. Two - Hai (hai) - "I have two books."
24. Three - Ba (bah) - "I have three cats."
25. Ten - Mười (moo-ee) - "I have ten dollars."

=== MÀU SẮC (COLORS) ===
26. Red - Đỏ (doh) - "The apple is red."
27. Blue - Xanh dương (sang doo-ung) - "The sky is blue."
28. Green - Xanh lá (sang lah) - "The grass is green."
29. Yellow - Vàng (vang) - "The sun is yellow."
30. White - Trắng (trang) - "The snow is white."
31. Black - Đen (den) - "The night is black."

=== GIA ĐÌNH (FAMILY) ===
32. Mother - Mẹ (meh) - "My mother is kind."
33. Father - Bố (boh) - "My father is tall."
34. Sister - Chị (chee) - "My sister is beautiful."
35. Brother - Anh (ang) - "My brother is strong."

=== NHÀ CỬA (HOME) ===
36. House - Nhà (nyah) - "This is my house."
37. Room - Phòng (fong) - "This is my room."
38. Door - Cửa (koo-ah) - "Please close the door."
39. Window - Cửa sổ (koo-ah soh) - "Open the window."
40. Table - Bàn (bahn) - "Put it on the table."
41. Chair - Ghế (geh) - "Sit on the chair."

=== GIÁO DỤC (EDUCATION) ===
42. Book - Sách (sahk) - "I love reading books."
43. Pen - Bút (boot) - "I need a pen to write."
44. School - Trường học (troo-ung hok) - "I go to school every day."
45. Teacher - Giáo viên (zow vee-en) - "My teacher is very kind."
46. Student - Học sinh (hok sing) - "I am a student."

=== GIAO THÔNG (TRANSPORTATION) ===
47. Car - Xe hơi (seh hoi) - "I drive a car."
48. Bicycle - Xe đạp (seh dahp) - "I ride a bicycle."
49. Bus - Xe buýt (seh boo-it) - "I take the bus to work."
50. Airplane - Máy bay (mai bai) - "I travel by airplane."

=== ĐỘNG VẬT (ANIMALS) ===
51. Dog - Con chó (kon choh) - "I have a pet dog."
52. Cat - Con mèo (kon meh-oh) - "I have a pet cat."
53. Bird - Con chim (kon cheem) - "The bird is singing."
54. Fish - Con cá (kon kah) - "I like eating fish."
55. Chicken - Con gà (kon gah) - "I eat chicken for dinner."

=== KINH DOANH (BUSINESS) ===
56. Money - Tiền (tee-en) - "I need money to buy food."
57. Work - Làm việc (lam vee-ek) - "I work every day."
58. Job - Công việc (kong vee-ek) - "I have a good job."
59. Market - Chợ (choh) - "I buy food at the market."
60. Store - Cửa hàng (koo-ah hang) - "I buy clothes at the store."

=== SỨC KHỎE (HEALTH) ===
61. Hospital - Bệnh viện (ben vee-en) - "I go to the hospital when I'm sick."
62. Doctor - Bác sĩ (bahk see) - "The doctor is very kind."
63. Medicine - Thuốc (too-ok) - "I take medicine when I'm sick."
64. Sick - Bệnh (ben) - "I feel sick today."
65. Healthy - Khỏe mạnh (kweh mang) - "I want to be healthy."

=== THỜI GIAN (TIME) ===
66. Today - Hôm nay (hom nai) - "Today is a beautiful day."
67. Tomorrow - Ngày mai (ngai mai) - "I will see you tomorrow."
68. Yesterday - Hôm qua (hom kwah) - "I went to school yesterday."
69. Week - Tuần (too-en) - "I work five days a week."
70. Month - Tháng (tang) - "I will travel next month."
71. Year - Năm (nam) - "Happy New Year!"
72. Morning - Buổi sáng (boo-ee sang) - "Good morning!"
73. Afternoon - Buổi chiều (boo-ee chee-oo) - "Good afternoon!"
74. Evening - Buổi tối (boo-ee toy) - "Good evening!"
75. Night - Đêm (dem) - "Good night!"

=== THIÊN NHIÊN (NATURE) ===
76. Sun - Mặt trời (mat troi) - "The sun is bright today."
77. Moon - Mặt trăng (mat trang) - "The moon is beautiful tonight."
78. Star - Ngôi sao (ngoi sow) - "I can see many stars tonight."
79. Rain - Mưa (moo-ah) - "It's raining today."
80. Wind - Gió (zoh) - "The wind is strong today."
81. Tree - Cây (kai) - "There are many trees in the park."
82. Flower - Hoa (hwah) - "The flowers are beautiful."
83. Mountain - Núi (noo-ee) - "I like climbing mountains."
84. River - Sông (song) - "The river is very long."
85. Sea - Biển (bee-en) - "I love swimming in the sea."

=== ĐỊA ĐIỂM (PLACES) ===
86. City - Thành phố (tang foh) - "I live in a big city."
87. Village - Làng (lang) - "My grandparents live in a village."
88. Park - Công viên (kong vee-en) - "I walk in the park every morning."
89. Restaurant - Nhà hàng (nyah hang) - "I eat at the restaurant."
90. Hotel - Khách sạn (kahk sahn) - "I stay at a hotel when I travel."
91. Airport - Sân bay (san bai) - "I go to the airport to travel."
92. Station - Ga (gah) - "I wait at the train station."
93. Bank - Ngân hàng (ngan hang) - "I go to the bank to get money."
94. Post office - Bưu điện (boo-ee dee-en) - "I send letters at the post office."
95. Library - Thư viện (too vee-en) - "I study at the library."
96. Museum - Bảo tàng (bow tang) - "I visit the museum to see art."
97. Theater - Rạp chiếu phim (rap chee-oo feem) - "I watch movies at the theater."
98. Beach - Bãi biển (bai bee-en) - "I swim at the beach."
99. Bridge - Cầu (kow) - "I cross the bridge to go home."
100. Street - Đường (doo-ung) - "I walk on the street."
